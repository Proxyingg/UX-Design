PROMOTIONS

Introducing Garden 4, a secret project that has been worked on by the Restaurant 4 staff for over a year. This garden uses hydroponics to produce fresh, organic vegetables for consumption.

From now until the end of September, Restaurant 4 is allowing customers to enter Garden 4, where the majority of our vegetables are sourced from.

Customers will be able to pick out vegetables of their choosing, and request how they want their vegetables served. Cost will be based on the vegetables chosen.
