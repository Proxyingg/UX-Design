ABOUT THE CAFE

Cafe 4 was founded in 1 by Little John in Singapore. Originally meant to be a standalone cafe, this restaurant now has made its way into the hearts of those who come and stay at Resort 4.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.

"We strive to serve excellent food at affordable rates"

-Little John, owner of Cafe 4.
