Facilities

Golf Course

The golf course sprawls across rolling hills, its lush fairways blending seamlessly with the natural landscape. Each hole offers a unique challenge, with strategically placed bunkers and water hazards enhancing the game. Whether you're an experienced golfer or a novice, this course offers a perfect blend of challenge and enjoyment in a picturesque setting.

Bowling Alley

The bowling alley is a vibrant and fun-filled venue, perfect for both casual outings and competitive play. It features well-maintained lanes with automated scoring systems, ensuring a seamless experience for all skill levels. Comfortable seating areas and a full-service snack bar offer a place to relax between games. The alley also hosts leagues and themed nights, making it a go-to destination for entertainment and socializing.

Swimming Pool

Designed with modern elegance, the pool features smooth azure tiles and is surrounded by lush greenery. Lounge chairs and stylish umbrellas offer spots for sunbathing and relaxation. The pool has a shallow end for wading and a deep end for diving, complemented by a bubbling jacuzzi for ultimate relaxation. This pool is a haven of peace, comfort, and joy, perfect for a refreshing dip or a serene escape.

Gym

The gym is a modern fitness haven equipped with state-of-the-art machines and free weights. Spacious and well-lit, it offers a variety of workout zones, including areas for cardio, strength training, and functional exercises. Group fitness classes and personal training sessions are available for all fitness levels. The gym also features clean locker rooms, complete with showers and sauna facilities, providing a comfortable and motivating environment for achieving fitness goals.
