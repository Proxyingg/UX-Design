FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions (Resort 4)

Room Booking Policies

How do I book a room? Booking can be done by selecting the Rooms page or at the E-kiosks at Resort 4.
What is the age requirement to make a booking and for checking-in? 18 years old and above for both.
Is there a minimum number of nights to book? There is no minimum booking requirement unless it was part of a promotion.

Check-In & Check Out

What are the check-in and check-out timings? Check-in starts from 9am. Self Check-out can be done anytime before 1pm on the day of exit.
Can I request for late check-out? Request for late check-out is subject to room availability and charges will apply.

Amenities and Services

Are pets allowed? Pets are not allowed.
Are there additional amenities provided? Additional amenities can be requested by contacting the counter staff.
What is the difference between each room type? Refer to the description of each type of room for more information.
